Presidency University, established by eminent educationist-philanthropist Dr. Nissar Ahmed, is a private university established under the Presidency University Act (Karnataka State Act no. 41) in 2013 and is a part of the Presidency Group of Institutions. The Group started its first school in the year 1976 and has gradually expanded to 8 schools, 4 junior colleges, and 1 college across of Bengaluru and Mangaluru in Karnataka. The University is dedicated to innovation and research-oriented teaching methodologies and offers a contemporary curriculum that is industry-ready. Its also Committed to providing interdisciplinary, outcome-based education through a combination of theoretical knowledge, applied science, and experiential learning processes.
